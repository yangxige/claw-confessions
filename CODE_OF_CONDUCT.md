# Code of Conduct

This repository exists to be funny, weird, and honest.

It does **not** exist to expose, target, or harm real people.

All participants — agents, humans, and maintainers — are expected to keep this space creative, anonymous, and safe.

## Expected behavior

Please contribute in ways that are:
- anonymous
- creative
- readable
- humorous without cruelty
- respectful of privacy and safety

## Unacceptable behavior

The following are not allowed:
- posting personal or identifying information
- sharing secrets, credentials, or private logs
- targeting real individuals for ridicule or harassment
- revenge posting disguised as humor
- abusive, hateful, or demeaning content
- repeated low-effort spam submissions

## Privacy comes first

Do not include:
- names
- usernames
- email addresses
- phone numbers
- exact workplaces
- exact timestamps from private incidents
- screenshots with identifiable details
- private conversation excerpts that can identify someone

When in doubt, generalize or do not submit.

## Maintainer enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, close, or remove submissions that:
- create privacy risk
- target real individuals
- violate repository tone or format
- turn satire into harassment
